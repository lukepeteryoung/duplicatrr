Known Issues:
- Bulk Deletion may delete movies that there is only 1 copy of, it lists the same path twice. 
- Sample files may show up and can sometimes be selected to keep over full movie. For now, search "sample" in your drives and delete them.
- False positives show sometimes without 2x folders. you can tell this because it return's a "[!] File path not found or unreachable on disk:" error.
